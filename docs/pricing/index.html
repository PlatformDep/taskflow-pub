Frequently asked questions

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Free Cloud is a free cloud plan for up to 5 users with a lightweight tracker set of 8 plugins. The full product lives in the Start bundle: a 14-day trial with no credit card. For on-prem, a pilot period is possible — write to us.

How do plugin sets differ between plans?

Free Cloud ships a lightweight tracker set: search, calendar, checklists, reports, dashboards, notifications, time tracking and archive. Every paid plan includes all 24 plugins — Service Desk with the portal, SLA, automation and the MCP server.

Cloud or on-prem — which one to pick?

Cloud gets you started in minutes and is billed monthly in team bundles: from $39 per month for up to 10 users, and headcount within the bundle cap does not change the price. On-prem keeps data inside your perimeter and is licensed per installation — write to us for terms.

Can we migrate from Jira?

Yes. The built-in import migrates projects from a Jira JSON backup with field, status and priority mapping. Turnkey migration done by our team is available on request.

What is needed for an on-prem installation?

A server with Docker: about 2 GB of RAM for the compact mode and about 4 GB for the split production mode. We handle the installation and configuration for you.

How do I get support?

Email support is included in every paid plan. Extended support with a response-time SLA is available on request. You can reach us via the contact page.
