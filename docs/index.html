Why TaskFlow

Not another kanban board — a replacement for the Jira + Jira Service Management bundle, in the cloud or inside your perimeter.

Plugin-first architecture

The core holds only base entities. Search, calendar, reports, automation and Service Desk are plugins built on GraphQL Federation and Module Federation: the system extends to fit your processes without forking the core.

Dev team + Service Desk

Issues, sprints and backlog for development plus a customer portal with queues, approvals and SLA — in one installation, no second system needed. Portal customers are free and unlimited.

Your data stays yours

On-prem installation on your server: data never leaves your perimeter. Or Cloud on our infrastructure — updates, backups and monitoring are on us.

No per-seat charges

The on-prem license is a flat price per installation, not per seat. Your team grows — the price does not.

Everything you expect from a tracker

Every major feature is a separate plugin: enable what you need, disable the rest.

Boards, sprints, Gantt

Drag-and-drop kanban, Scrum with sprints and backlog, lists and a Gantt chart. Multiple boards per project.

Custom workflows

Per-project statuses and transitions with a visual workflow editor.

Service Desk

Customer portal, request types, queues, approval chains, knowledge base and CSAT ratings.

SLA policies

Goals and timers aware of business hours and timezones, automatic breach detection.

Automation

Trigger → condition → action rules with a visual builder, events processed in real time.

MCP for AI agents

A built-in MCP server: Claude, Cursor or your own AI agent works with issues without leaving your perimeter. 11 tools, read and write modes, a call log.

Reports & dashboards

Velocity, Burndown, Created vs Resolved and Jira-style dashboards with widgets provided by plugins.

Notifications

Real-time in-app notifications (SSE), email and a Telegram bot, watchers and mentions.

Permissions & security

RBAC with permission schemes, resource grants, two-factor authentication (TOTP) and a full audit log.

Jira import

Migrate projects from a Jira JSON backup with field, status and priority mapping.

Unique feature

Release Waves: one issue — 25 brands

Rolling one task out to dozens of brands or clients? Each brand gets its own workflow and its own status inside a single issue, simultaneously.

  • Per-brand statuses: one brand is “in print” while another is “in approval”
  • A dedicated workflow per brand — no issue cloning
  • Neither Plane, nor Huly, nor OpenProject can do this
