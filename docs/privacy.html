2. What data is processed

The Operator processes the following user data:

  • the e-mail address provided when registering an account;
  • a name or nickname, if the user specifies one in the profile;
  • payment metadata: amount, date and transaction ID (full bank card details are not transferred to and not processed by the Operator);
  • technical data: IP address, browser and device information, cookies and similar technologies required for the Service to function.

3. Purposes of processing

The data is processed for the following purposes:

  • creating and maintaining the account, providing access to the Service;
  • accepting payments, fiscalization and sending fiscal receipts in accordance with Federal Law No. 54-FZ;
  • technical support and communication with the user regarding the Service;
  • notifying about changes to the Service, plans and legal documents;
  • complying with the requirements of applicable law.

4. Legal grounds

The processing is based on the agreement concluded with the user (the User Agreement and Public Offer), the consent of the user, and the requirements of applicable law.

5. Transfer of data to third parties

To accept payments and send fiscal receipts, user data (e-mail address, amount and transaction ID) is transferred to YooMoney NBCO LLC (the YooKassa service).

The data may be processed by technical contractors of the Operator (hosting, infrastructure) to the extent necessary for the Service to function.

Personal data is not sold and is not transferred to any other third parties, except in cases required by law.

6. Storage and protection

The Operator takes the necessary legal, organizational and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure and destruction.

The data is stored for the lifetime of the account and, after its deletion, for the periods established by law.
